2017 Autumn Classic International

This event was held in Montreal, Canada. It was my first competition in Canada and it was super exciting because many Olympians were also competing in the event, including Yuzuru Hanyu from Japan. I skated very well at this event and it remains one of my favorites to this day. I competed in the Senior level event but there was also a Junior level event as well.
